Changes made in Potato Corner website

Events Page Layout and Booking Form Development

Potato Corner wants to promote their events packages: 'Regular Package' and 'Large Package' with additional offer that includes new products. By clicking on your selected package will redirect you to your selected package page then if you click 'Book Now' it will redirect you again to a booking form where you enter your contact information and event information. Homepage

  • Homepage Slider

  • 4-Column Article Category Section or The Grid Module

    It was compose of four recently uploaded articles sorted by category. The following categories are Magazine, Featured, Community and News & Updates.

  • 3-Column Video Section or The Post Video

    This is where recently uploaded videos show and all video came from our YouTube channel.

Archives or Category Pages

There are five categories that shows different type of articles: Magazine, Featured, Community, News & Updates and Videos. Each category page or archive page has a featured article on top which is the recently uploaded article, followed by articles at the bottom.

Inside Article

  • Featured Image

    This is the cover image of an article. It shows at the top of an article page.

  • Share to Social Media

    You can share the article to social media like in Facebook and Instagram or copy the link instead. Every article was optimize when shared to social media.

  • Embed Social Media Posts/Feeds

    This will allow you to embed posts from Facebook, feeds from Instagram or a tweet from X.

  • Read More Section

    At the bottom of an article there are three recommended articles to read next.

  • Auto Load Posts

    After the Read More section, another article will load. If you keep scrolling down more articles will load.
